'Centre for Automotive Research' Promotional Materials

Promotional retractable banners and informational pamphlet

The Brief

The goal of this project was to design a series of banners that promote brand awareness and generate research investments in CAR and an informational pamphlet on CAR's research initiatives.

Tools

Adobe Photoshop

Adobe Illustrator

Timeline

2 weeks

Designers

Robyn Chmelyk & Elyssa Biringer

Business Objectives

To promote the Centre for Automotive Research (CAR) program to industry professionals in order to generate research investment in CAR.

To increase general brand awareness of CAR within the local and global automotive industry through memorable promotional materials - that conform to the current CAR corporate identity.

Rationale

The visual language for the materials is drawn from photo taken of research activities and equipment in CAR - a department of the Lassonde School of Engineering - and the triangular windows of Lassonde's Bergeron Centre.

The primary research directives of CAR are 'Innovation & Engineering' and 'Research & Development', thus we created two sets of banners that would promote CAR's brand within the automotive industry and provide wayfinding and signage for events put on by CAR. Additionally, the banners are designed to function as sets of 2 or 4.
