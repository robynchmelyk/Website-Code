'Growr' Mobile App

Mobile app design, prototype, and illustrations

The Brief

The goal of this project was to produce a digital product that promotes ecological sustainability, mental wellness, and uses smart technologies to some degree.

Tools

Adobe Illustrator

Figma

Timeline

36 hours

Designer

Robyn Chmelyk

The Problem

We consume gas at an alarming rate worldwide.

In Canada alone, we use 111 million litres of gas per day and gas usage accounts for 38% of all of Canada’s CO2 emissions.

The concept is gamify reducing our overall gas consumption and carbon footprint.

The Solution

The app will use the gas consumption data collected by the user’s smart vehicle to visualize their gas usage efficiency.

Additionally, eco-friendly drivers want to be recognized. Local leaderboards of eco-friendly drivers will be used to incentivize eco-conscious drivers through comparison.
