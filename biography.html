I'm a lifelong learner who thrives on challenge and producing user-centered, socially-conscious design products and experiences.

Exploring user behaviours to draw key design insights is my favourite part of the design process.

My education as a graphic designer began in 2016 at the Alberta University of the Arts in Calgary, AB, where I learned design fundamentals, drawing for design, glassblowing, and jewelry crafting. Additionally, I was surrounded by numerous concept-driven, McLuhanist installation artists who taught me how to develop high-concept work.

After two years at AUArts I transfered to the York/Sheridan Design Program in Toronto, ON and have been living in Toronto ever since. I plan to graduate in the spring of 2021!

I have been a freelance designer for the past 6 years, working on a variety of traditional promotional materials for clients - including logos, social media graphics, banner ads, motion graphic ads, business cards, flyers, posters, websites and more.

Working in the customer service industry for the last 14 years - including 3 years experience serving and managing servers - has honed my skills at developing relationships easily with clients, users, and team members.
