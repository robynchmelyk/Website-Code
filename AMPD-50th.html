The Problem

The Design Lab was approached by the School of the Arts, Media, Performance & Design (AMPD) to create a commemorative campaign for their 50th anniversary.

The creative campaign needed to feature a give-away item for alumni and guests of the celebration event for AMPD's 50th anniversary. The campaign needed to reflect back on the 50 years of history, creativity and transformation of AMPD, as well as to promote the faculty, reach out to alumni, and encourage more people to support arts education.

Goals

The client had three main goals for the campaign:

Incorporate All AMPD Departments

There are approximately 40 different departments that fit under the umbrella of AMPD.

Showcases AMPD's Designers

The client wanted to showcase the talents and creativity of the Design Lab's designers (us).

Incorporate Existing Brand

There was already an established brand identity for AMPD's 50th Anniversary, so we needed to make work that supplemented it.

Rationale

The campaign uses important figures and imagery from art history.

The overall creative direction of the campaign drew from traditional and modern design techniques and images to produce designs that feel both traditional and modern.

The Solutions

Arts Postcard

The 'Arts' postcard needed to represent the Visual Arts, Art History, and Computational Arts departments simultaneously. Blendr was used to 3D render a shape and then used to fill the entire composition of the postcard, reminiscent of a modern art painting.

Media Postcard

The 'Media' postcard visualizes the focus of the Media department - Cinema Studies - through using an image of a vintage camera, applying a duotone, and styling the postcard like a 1960s movie poster.

Performance Postcard

The 'Performance' postcard also draws from the historical influences of the Performance department. Image traces of vintage illustrations of historically significant dancers, musicians, and thespians are used to create a background texture. The word 'Performance' is repeated to emphasize the group aspect of performance.

Design Postcard

The 'Design' postcard visualizes the field of Design's current existence; a blending of print and digital methods. Gradients, image traces, beveled text, pixelated shapes, and grids are used to create an effect that merges print and digital aesthetics.

Explanatory Postcard

The 'Explanation Card' was created to explain the purpose of the campaign to attendees of AMPD's 50th Anniversary Celebration event.

Concepts

We pitched the client on other creative, such as a logo variation and animation, as well as other promotional giveaways.

Ultimately, the client didn't feel that they were able to implement the animation accross their network, while the promotional giveaways were not feasible within the current budget.
